What is Bid2Win?

• BID2WIN is a bidding competition that is enabled via mobile where the bidder can win by becoming the lowest and unique bidder. Customers can bid Daily / Weekly / Monthly.

• The competition will be open to the subscribers on broadcast media or via SMS where they can get to know about the gifts to be won as well as the winner’s details.

Activation

• Bid2Win Vouchers : Type REG (space) BW & SMS to 3947

• Bid2Win Devices : Type REG (space) BD & SMS to 3947

How to play

• Type BW space your Lowest Unique Bid and SMS to 3947

o Vouchers: Eg- Type BW (space) 56 and SMS to 3947
o Devices: Eg- Type BD (space) 56 and SMS to 3947

Deactivation

• Bid2Win Vouchers : Type UNREG (space) BW & SMS to 3947

• Bid2Win Devices : Type UNREG (space) BD & SMS to 3947

How the Game works

• Each bidder is notified with the status of the competition. If the customer holds a unique and lowest bid, he will be notified via SMS. If the customers bid become non-unique or higher than the lowest bid customer will get a SMS notification to encourage bidding more.

Customer Charging for Bid2Win

• All bid gamers will be charged Rs.6+tax per day for 4 bidding chances. If the customer needs to place more bids, the customer will be charged an additional Rs.6+tax and get an additional 4 bidding chances.
